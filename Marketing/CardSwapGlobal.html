Our Ethical Commitment

Corporate social responsibility drives us to source ethically—all Child Labourers™ are exclusively hired from underprivileged communities.*

We use up to 7.3% recycled materials across our packaging lines, and provide our team with generous 30-second micro-breaks™ every 6 hours.

*We pay these families $5-12, which is competitive in their regions.

This is what responsible business looks like™.

Strategic Initiatives

  • Project Hope™ – A quarterly donation of misprinted cards to struggling regions.
  • Digital Futures™ – Transforming cardboard into carbon-intensive NFTs since 2023.
