Usage guidelines

Keep the Donut identity consistent and recognizable.

  • Do not distort, rotate, or add drop shadows to the logo marks.
  • Maintain clear space equal to the inner ring height around each mark.
  • Use the vector logo only for scaling and production. Do not edit paths, colors, or effects.
  • Use Donut Lilac and Nightfold as the primary contrast pair.
  • Headlines should feel bold, cinematic, and short.
  • For press use, pair logos with the brand snapshot above.
