About Fr. Gregory Echegwo

Fr. Gregory Chukwuma Echegwo SDB was born in Nigeria on July 23, 1988. As the seventh of eight children, he grew up with a strong sense of faith, spending six years living with a priest while completing high school. In 2008, he joined the Salesians of Don Bosco, inspired by their mission and service to the young.

In 2013, he moved to the United Kingdom as a missionary, studying theology at St. Mary's University and Allen Hall Seminary. After years of study, he was ordained a priest on June 26, 2021, at Sacred Heart Church, Battersea.
