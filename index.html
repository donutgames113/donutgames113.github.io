Who is Donut?

Donut is a solo Roblox developer behind Playzero, making Roblox games, Lua systems, UI, graphics, branding, environments, and web projects.

What is Playzero?

Playzero is my Roblox development brand. It is where I put original projects, gameplay ideas, visual identity work, and public releases.

Does Donut make games for other people?

Yes. I make my own projects, but I can also help other people with Roblox development, building, Lua scripting, UI, graphics, branding, and prototypes.
